Det lille fyrtårn

Det Lille Fyrtårn, or The Little Lighthouse, is a charming landmark located in Marselisborg Harbor, Aarhus, Denmark. Small and picturesque, it adds a unique touch to the bustling marina, blending maritime heritage with modern harbor life.

Though not a functional beacon for navigation, its quaint design and coastal ambiance make it a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike, offering a perfect backdrop for a peaceful stroll or a moment of reflection by the sea.
